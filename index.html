FreeCodeCamp Timestamp Micro-Service

Usage

Enter any UNIX or human-readable date as a parameter at the end of this URL, and receive back the UNIX date and the natural date together in JSON format. For example:

            https://limitless-bastion-34597.herokuapp.com/687830400
                                    OR
           https://limitless-bastion-34597.herokuapp.com/October%2019,%201991

Entering either of these will produce the following output:

             {"unix":687830400,"natural":"October 19, 1991"}
